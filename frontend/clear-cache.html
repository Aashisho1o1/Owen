Why clear cache?
If you're seeing old content (like the Kenji detective story) that shouldn't be there, clearing your browser cache will remove any stored data and give you a fresh start.

Quick Actions:

Manual Browser Cache Clear:

  • Chrome/Edge: Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
  • Firefox: Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
  • Safari: Cmd+Option+E
After clearing cache:
1. Refresh the DOG Writer page
2. You may need to log in again
3. Any unsaved work will be lost
4. The editor should now be empty by default
